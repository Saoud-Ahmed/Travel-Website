Chilam Joshi Festival & Beyond

13 Days - 12 Nights | Explore Pakistan's Cultural Marvels

Departure : Group Trips - | Private depending on bookings

Fitness Level 2: 6-7 hours of physical activity per day | 1500-15,535 ft above sea level.

Culture, Nature Discovery & Sightseeing

Introduction

Crossroads Adventure invites you on an unforgettable adventure through the cultural marvels of Pakistan with our meticulously crafted tour, featuring the iconic Chilam Joshi Festival in the enchanting Kalash Valley. From the bustling streets of Islamabad to the serene valleys of Swat and Chitral, immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of history, tradition, and natural beauty that this remarkable country has to offer. Join us on a 13-day journey filled with awe-inspiring landscapes, ancient archaeological sites, and vibrant cultural celebrations. Don't miss the chance to witness the Chilam Joshi Festival, celebrated by the Kalash people from May 8th to May 10th, showcasing captivating dances and rituals that have endured for centuries. Get ready to create lasting memories as you explore Pakistan's hidden gems and uncover the secrets of its diverse heritage.

Highlights

Chilam Joshi Festival

Experience the vibrant festivities of the Chilam Joshi Festival, a three-day celebration of Kalash culture, featuring traditional dances and rituals.

Historical Treasures

Explore ancient Buddhist monasteries at Takht-i-Bahi and immerse yourself in Gandharan art at the Taxila Museum

Scenic Drives

Traverse the breathtaking landscapes of Swat, Chitral, and Hunza valleys, with panoramic views of snow-capped mountains and crystal-clear lakes.

Cultural Immersion

Engage with local communities in Chitral and Hunza, visit bustling bazaars, and delve into the fascinating history and traditions of Pakistan

Iconic Landmarks

Discover architectural marvels like Baltit and Altit forts, witness the majesty of Rakaposhi viewpoint, and stroll through the ancient Silk Route remnants.

Itinerary

Arrive in Islamabad and embark on a captivating city tour to acquaint yourself with the cultural and historical essence of Pakistan's capital.

Begin your journey with a visit to the ancient Buddhist monastery of Takht-i-Bahi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Then, proceed to the picturesque Swat Valley, known for its breathtaking landscapes.

Travel to Chitral Valley, a full-day drive offering mesmerizing views, including the iconic Lawari Tunnel. Overnight stay in Chitral city.

Journey to Bumburet Valley with a stopover at Tirich-Mir viewpoint. Explore the enchanting beauty of Bumburet Valley before retiring for the night.

Immerse yourself in the vibrant Chilam Joshi Festival celebrated by the Kalash people, featuring captivating dances and cultural performances. Return to Chitral town for overnight stay.

Spend a full day exploring Chitral town, including visits to the Chitral museum and local markets, soaking in the rich heritage and traditions.

Embark on an adventurous drive to Gupis Valley via the scenic Shandur Pass and Phander Valley, relishing the awe-inspiring landscapes along the way.

Continue your journey through picturesque Ghizer Valley and Gilgit, with captivating stopovers at ancient Silk Route remnants and Rakaposhi viewpoint.

Explore the majestic Baltit and Altit forts, stroll through Karimabad bazaar, and enjoy panoramic views of Hunza Valley from Duikar/Eagles' Nest. Overnight stay in Karimabad.

Embark on a full-day adventure exploring Attabad Lake, Hussaini Suspension Bridge, Borith Lake, Passu Cones, and charming villages of Gulmit and Ghukin.

Enjoy a scenic drive back to Bisham/Naran along the iconic Karakoram Highway, soaking in the grandeur of the surrounding mountainscapes.

En route to Islamabad, visit the Taxila Museum, showcasing a remarkable collection of Gandharan art spanning centuries. Return to Islamabad for overnight stay.

Conclude your adventure with a morning city tour of Islamabad, followed by transfer to the airport for your international departure, carrying cherished memories of Pakistan's cultural wonders.
